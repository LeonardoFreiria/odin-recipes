Coxinha

Description

Coxinha is a Brazilian snack, of SÃ£o Paulo origin, also common in Portugal, made with wheat flour dough and chicken broth, which involves a filling made with seasoned chicken meat, cheese, sausages or various other types of flavors.

Ingredients

  • 2 glasses of water
  • 1 glass of milk
  • 1 tablespoon of margarine
  • 1 cube of chicken broth
  • salt to taste
  • 3 cups of wheat

Steps

  1. Put the water, milk, margarine, broth and salt in a saucepan and mix until boiling.
  2. Lower the heat and add the wheat at once.
  3. Stir until smooth and homogeneous.
  4. Stuff it any way you like.

Feijoada

Description

Feijoada is a common designation given to culinary dishes from Portuguese-speaking regions and countries.

It consists of a bean stew, usually with meat, and almost always accompanied with rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg of black beans
  • 100 g of dried meat
  • 70 g of pig's ear
  • 70 g of pork tail
  • 70 g of pig's foot
  • 100 g of pork chops
  • 50 g of pork tenderloin
  • 100 g of paio
  • 150 g of Portuguese sausage

Steps

  1. 2 large onions chopped
  2. 1 packet of chopped green onions
  3. 3 leaves of bay leaf
  4. 6 cloves of garlic
  5. Black pepper to taste
  6. 1 or 2 oranges
  7. 40 ml of drip
  8. Salt if you need
